### 🧩 Engineering complex systems

I build software that helps organizations understand, optimize, and improve complex systems. My work lives at the intersection of:

`Software Engineering` · `Operations Research` · `Supply Chain Engineering` · `Industrial Engineering` · `Simulation` · `Data Science` · `Network Optimization` · `Sustainability`

Rather than write software just for software's sake, I care about engineering systems that support **better decisions** — whether that's in logistics, manufacturing, transportation, or industrial operations. If it helps someone make a smarter call with their resources, I'm interested.
